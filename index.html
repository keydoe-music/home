Key Doe

Key Doe is an artist out of Missouri. He dropped his first ever single "Alone" in highschool that gained a lot of attention from his classmates. With a follow up EP called "On My Last" that shares his loss of his mom, his dad being locked up and also losing a best friend to gun violence. The looks of it Key Doe doesn't plan on stopping his journey any time soon. Every song is filled with raw pain that leaves people with chills.
